# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis & Retention Dashboard

### Python · SQL · Power BI · Excel

This project presents an interactive **Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** to analyze customer behavior, churn trends, subscription patterns, revenue impact, and customer retention performance.

The dashboard helps businesses identify high-risk customers, monitor churn KPIs, understand customer demographics, and make data-driven decisions to improve customer retention and business growth.

# 📉 Trends Analysis

## 👨‍💼 High Churn Customer Segments

Higher churn is observed among:

- Month-to-Month Contract Customers  
- Customers with High Monthly Charges  
- Customers without Technical Support  

This highlights the need for targeted retention strategies.

---

## 💳 Contract & Payment Trends

Customers using:

- Electronic Check Payment  
- Short-Term Contracts  

show comparatively higher churn percentages.

---

## 😊 Customer Retention Insights

The dashboard reveals strong relationships between:

- Service Quality  
- Contract Type  
- Support Availability  
- Pricing Structure  

and customer retention performance.

---

# 💡 Key Insights

- Month-to-Month customers contribute the highest churn rate  
- Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to churn  
- Electronic Check users show higher churn percentages  
- Customers without tech support are at higher churn risk  
- Fiber Optic internet users experience higher churn  
- Long-term contracts improve customer retention
